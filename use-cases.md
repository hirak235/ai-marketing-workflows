# Real-World AI Marketing Use Cases

This document outlines how AI workflows from this repository are applied in real business scenarios.

## 1. Paid Media Optimization at Scale

**Problem:**  
High ad spend with unclear inefficiencies across campaigns.

**Solution:**  
Used AI prompts to analyze campaign performance data and identify:
- Wasted spend segments  
- Underperforming creatives  
- Scaling opportunities  

**Impact:**  
Improved CAC efficiency and accelerated testing velocity.
